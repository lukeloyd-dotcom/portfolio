Who I Am

Curious by nature.

I'm a junior in San Antonio who got genuinely interested in how businesses work — not from a class, but from actually trying to run one. TheLocalGrid started as an idea to help local businesses get a better foothold online. It's small, it's a work in progress, and I've learned a lot from it.

Outside of that, I serve as president of my school's DECA chapter, which has pushed me to think seriously about strategy, leadership, and financial analysis. I'm hoping to study finance or accounting in college and keep building from there.
